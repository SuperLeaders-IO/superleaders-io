# Professional Summary: Iranetta Rayborn Wright

## Overview
Iranetta Rayborn Wright is a highly accomplished educational leader with nearly 25 years of experience in public education. Known for her strategic vision and commitment to equity, she has served as a Superintendent and senior executive in some of the nation's largest school districts. She is currently the CEO of The Wright Way Leadership Group, offering consulting and leadership development.

## Key Roles & Experience
*   **CEO, The Wright Way Leadership Group:** Providing expert consulting and leadership development.
*   **Superintendent, Cincinnati Public Schools (OH):** Led Ohio's third-largest and fastest-growing large-city district (approx. 36,000 students).
*   **Deputy Superintendent of Schools, Detroit Public Schools Community District (MI):** Managed operations for roughly 50,000 students and initiated key school turnaround efforts.
*   **Chief of Schools, Duval County Public Schools (FL):** Oversaw services for ~117,000 students; improved 36 low-performing campuses to remove them from state sanctions.
*   **Various Roles:** Effectively served as a teacher, assistant principal, principal, and regional superintendent.

## Achievements & Recognition
*   **School Turnaround:** Successfully improved the lowest-performing high school in Florida from an 'F' to a 'B' grade and prevented state sanctions for numerous schools.
*   **Awards:**
    *   Champion of Education (City Year).
    *   Innovator in Education (United Negro College Fund).
    *   Woman of Excellence (Michigan Chronicle).
*   **Advocacy:** Dedicated to empowering Black male educators through initiatives like the Black Male Educator Summit.
