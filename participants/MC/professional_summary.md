# Professional Summary: Dr. Michael Conner, Ed.D.

## Overview
Dr. Michael T. Conner is a distinguished educator, entrepreneur, author, and speaker. He is the CEO and Founder of Agile Evolutionary Group, an organization dedicated to providing objective, data-driven solutions to businesses and transforming educational systems. With nearly two decades of experience, his career has spanned from the classroom to the superintendency, characterized by a focus on educational transformation, innovation, and equity.

## Key Roles & Experience
*   **CEO & Founder, Agile Evolutionary Group:** Leading organizational transformation through data-driven solutions.
*   **Superintendent of Schools, Middletown Public Schools (CT):**Led the district to recognition as an AASA/SPN National Innovation and Transformational District.
*   **Chief Academic Officer:** Norwalk Public Schools, CT.
*   **Assistant Superintendent of Teaching and Learning:** Willimantic, CT.
*   **Principal:** Augusta Lewis Troup School, New Haven, CT.
*   **Educator:** Started career as a fourth-grade teacher.
