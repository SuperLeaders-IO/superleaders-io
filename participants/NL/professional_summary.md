# Professional Summary: Nicole Long

## Overview
Nicole Long is a strategic corporate leader with nearly two decades of experience in healthcare technology and service delivery. Currently serving as the Vice President of CXT Service Delivery for Customer Success at Lyric, she is passionate about building team and brand confidence to drive success. Her career spans significant leadership roles at major organizations like Change Healthcare and McKesson, where she focused on client services, operational excellence, and enterprise program management.

## Key Roles & Experience
*   **Vice President, CXT Service Delivery for Customer Success, Lyric:** Responsible for CXT Services and Support operations, including service delivery, shared services, customer support, and financial/contracting operations.
*   **Area Vice President, Change Healthcare:** Led national and regional service delivery initiatives.
*   **Director, Client Services, Change Healthcare:** Managed client services and operations.
*   **Enterprise Program Executive, McKesson:** Oversaw enterprise-level programs and initiatives.
*   **Prior Experience:** Held leadership positions at Picis and GE Healthcare.

## Expertise & Focus Areas
*   **Service Delivery & Customer Success:** Expert in managing complex service operations and ensuring client satisfaction.
*   **Organizational Management:** Skilled in leading diverse teams and departments to achieve strategic goals.
